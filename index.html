Circus Rudá with spectacle Marabá

The Circo Rudá arrives in Brasilia with the spectacle Marabá, putting emotion and bringing joy to all who contemplate.

Sunday is Theater Day

Sunday is Theater Day, is a project that aims to provide entertainment and culture for the whole family with free performances of children's plays.

When: Every Sunday

Time: At 3PM

Place: Eva Herz Theater - Livraria Cultura do Iguatemi Brasilia.

Let's skate on ice?

An ice skating rink of 210 m² is installed on the 2nd Floor of ParkShopping Brasilia. The ice rink is a children's adventure and is a fun option for the whole family.

When: Monday to Saturday

Place: ParkShopping Brasilia
